Welcome to HackNC, The Grand Carolina Hackathon!

Our aim is to make HackNC a place for hackers of all skill levels to come and broaden their talents. Throughout the entire event, hands-on demos, lectures, and tutorials will be available, run by sponsors, professionals, and professors on a range of topics from introductory concepts to the latest groundbreaking research.

During your time here, you will have access to all of the following amenities: food, internet, swag, mentors, and anything else you need to succeed. If this sounds too overwhelming, don’t worry. HackNC is not only for advanced programmers at the university level; it also provides computer science amuse-bouche to help you find what you are interested in. Whether you are a high schooler, a college student, or a local professional, on beginner or hard mode, you are welcome to come and learn all the ints and outs of computer science.

Bring your curiosity and creativity and be ready to do something new at HackNC!

Frequently asked questions

Can I attend?

High school, undergraduate, and graduate students are welcome to hack. Professionals are welcome to join as mentors, sponsors, or lecturers.

What's going to happen?

A weekend of fun and learning. You'll become a better programmer, meet new people, and make cool stuff. You'll also have the chance to learn a lot and get loads of free stuff!

What about my team?

Be yourself; sign up as an individual! You can create your team with whoever you like once you arrive.

What can we hack?

HackNC is both a software and hardware hackathon. Code for web, mobile, desktop, and hack away at hardware to your heart's content.

Can we build off existing code?

You may use all the publicly available code and APIs that you want but the code you will be judged on at the end of the hackathon must be new.

Will there be food?

Don’t worry. We’ll provide six full meals throughout the hackathon and there will be plenty of snacks in between. You won’t be hungry.

What about sleep?

Traditionally, sleep at hackathons comes in the form of a blanket and pillow. There will be a room designated for sleeping during Saturday night if you get tired. If you'd prefer a hotel room, the Carolina Inn is only 200 feet away (although very expensive).

What hardware will be provided?

MLH is providing the full hardware lab. In addition we will have soldering and assorted components, Arduinos, Raspberry Pis, and more Pebbles than you can wear on four limbs...

Do I need a computer?

If you plan on hacking then bring a device with internet access, preferrably a laptop (or desktop + monitor + mouse + keyboard if you really want to).

Will there be travel reimbursements?

Yes! Please state that you are seeking a travel reimbursement on your application. We will let you know if you are approved when you are admitted.

What are the admission requirements?

Anyone can attend HackNC. There are no admission requirements. The only limitation is the amount of food we can serve over the weekend.

Will I fit in?

Yes, you will! HackNC and UNC Computer Science is committed to making computer science a more diverse field. That's why we are requiring applications this year. We want to make this the most inclusive HackNC yet.

What about...?

Email us with any of your questions.
