More information

Can I attend?

High school, undergraduate, and graduate students are welcome to hack. Professionals are welcome to join as mentors and sponsors.

What's going to happen?

A 24 hour period of hacking during which you will become a better programmer, meet new people, and make cool stuff. You'll have the chance to learn a lot and get loads of free swag!

What can we hack?

HackNC is both a software and hardware hackathon. Code for web, mobile, desktop, and hack away at hardware to your heart's content.

Can we build off of existing code?

The purpose of the event is to see what you can do in the time you're given. You can use all the APIs you want but the code you will be judged on must be new.

Will there be food?

Don’t worry. We’ll provide meals throughout the hackathon and there will be plenty of snacks in between. You won’t be hungry.

What about sleep?

Traditionally, sleep at hackathons comes in the form of a blanket and pillow. There will be a room designated for sleeping during Saturday night if you get tired. If you'd prefer a hotel room, the Carolina Inn is only 200 feet away (although very expensive).

What hardware will be provided?

MLH is providing the full hardware lab. In addition, we have more Pebbles, Arduinos, and Raspberry Pis than you could imagine.

What about travel reimbursements

We are reimbursing travel up to a maximum amount for everyone who is admitted. Please specify your travel status when you apply.

What else?

What else?! We will have a video game lounge, tons of mentors, info-sessions, a hardware lab, free swag, and anything else you could possibly dream of...
