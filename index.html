Welcome to Pearl Hacks!

Pearl Hacks is an all-female hackathon aimed towards creating an encouraging environment for women of all skill levels to pursue their interests in technology. Hosted at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, for 24 hours you will build projects with peers and mentors about topics that spark your interests. Whether you are a master in heels and Java or you have yet to write your first "Hello World" program, we welcome you all. Look forward to participating in workshops for aspiring and experienced programmers. If advancing your knowledge of computer science isn't enough, we'll have plenty of free food, free shirts, and free swag. What's stopping you? Get ready for a weekend with phenomenal women you will never forget at Pearl Hacks!

Frequently asked questions

Can I attend?

High school, undergraduate, and graduate students are welcome to hack. Professionals are welcome to join as mentors, sponsors, or lecturers.

What is it?

A weekend of fun and learning. You'll become a better programmer, meet fantastic ladies, and make cool stuff. You'll also have the chance to learn a lot and get loads of free stuff!

Will there be food?

Don’t worry. We’ll provide five full meals throughout the hackathon and there will be plenty of snacks in between. You won’t be hungry.

Can I use old code?

You may use all the publicly available code and APIs that you want but the code you will be judged on at the end of the hackathon must be new.

Registration Decisions

Registration decisions will be announced on February 28th. Afterwards, we will review applications on a case-by-case basis.

What about sleep?

Bring your own pillow and bedding. There will be a sleeping space available Friday and Saturday nights. If you'd prefer a hotel room, the Carolina Inn is only 200 feet away (although very expensive).

Hardware?

MLH is providing the full hardware lab. In addition we will have soldering and assorted components, Arduinos, Raspberry Pis, and more Pebbles than you can wear on four limbs...

What about my team?

Be yourself; sign up as an individual! You can create your team with whoever you like, up to four people, once you arrive.

Travel costs?

See our travel policy for more information.

How do I get in?

Anyone can attend HackNC. Just thoughtfully fill out the application. The only limitation is the amount of food we can serve over the weekend.

Will I fit in?

Yes, you will! HackNC and UNC Computer Science is committed to making computer science a more diverse field. We are working to make this the most inclusive HackNC yet.
