Welcome to HackNC!

HackNC is a weekend for students of all skill levels to broaden their talents. This is a weekend devoted to coding. Your challenge is to make an awesome project in just a weekend. You will have access to hands-on demos and workshops from our sponsors as well as exciting talks about the awesome things happening right now with computer science. If this sounds too overwhelming, don’t worry. HackNC is not only for advanced programmers at the university level; it also provides computer science amuse-bouche to help you find what you are interested in. Whether you are a high schooler, a college student, on beginner or hard mode, you are welcome to come and learn all the ints and outs of computer science.

During your time here, you will have access to food, internet, swag, mentors, and anything else you need to succeed. HackNC is beginner friendly and provides an atmosphere for innovation. So with that, bring your curiosity and creativity and be ready to do something new at HackNC!

Frequently asked questions

Can I attend?

High school, undergraduate, and graduate students are welcome to hack. Professionals are welcome to join as mentors, sponsors, or lecturers.

What's going to happen?

A weekend of fun and learning. You'll become a better programmer, meet new people, and make cool stuff. You'll also have the chance to learn a lot and get loads of free stuff!

Will there be food?

Don’t worry. We’ll provide six full meals throughout the hackathon and there will be plenty of snacks in between. You won’t be hungry.

What can we hack?

HackNC is both a software and hardware hackathon. Code for web, mobile, desktop, and hack away at hardware to your heart's content.

Can I use old code?

You may use all the publicly available code and APIs that you want but the code you will be judged on at the end of the hackathon must be new.

Do I need a computer?

If you plan on hacking then bring a device with internet access, preferrably a laptop (or desktop + monitor + mouse + keyboard if you really want to).

What about sleep?

Bring your own pillow and bedding. There will be a sleeping space available Friday and Saturday nights. If you'd prefer a hotel room, the Carolina Inn is only 200 feet away (although very expensive).

Will there be hardware?

MLH is providing the full hardware lab. In addition we will have soldering and assorted components, Arduinos, Raspberry Pis, and more Pebbles than you can wear on four limbs...

What about my team?

Be yourself; sign up as an individual! You can create your team with whoever you like once you arrive.

Travel reimbursements?

Yes! Please state that you are seeking a travel reimbursement on your application. We will let you know if you are approved when you are admitted.

How do I get admitted?

Anyone can attend HackNC. There are no admission requirements. The only limitation is the amount of food we can serve over the weekend.

Will I fit in?

Yes, you will! HackNC and UNC Computer Science is committed to making computer science a more diverse field. We are working to make this the most inclusive HackNC yet.

What about...?

Email us with any of your questions.
