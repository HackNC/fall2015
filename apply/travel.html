HackNC Travel Grants

HackNC is providing the following types of travel grants for students traveling from more than 50 miles away. Please note that you are required to actively participate in the event and submit a project in order to receive a grant.

< Back to application

Carpools

A carpool consists of 3 or more students intending to participate in HackNC.

Every carpool is automatically approved for a grant equivalent to the cost of gas, not to exceed $0.10/mile. Limit one per carpool. In order to receive a grant you must RSVP (see link in your email) and provide receipts of all gas purchases for which you request reimbursement. Receipts should be sent by email to gasreceipts@hacknc.com after HackNC. Grants will be distributed after the event.

Large Vans

Contact us if you are looking to coordinate a van of 6 or more. Email vansRus@hacknc.com. The process of receiving your grant is similar to normal carpools.

Other transit options

HackNC has a limited number of $50 grants available to supplement the cost of bus/train/plane tickets. Email us at grantMeTravel@hacknc.com before booking to confirm availability.

Questions?

If you have any questions please email us at travel@hacknc.com.
