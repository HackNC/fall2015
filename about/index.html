Cheat Codes

Want to get the most out of your experience at a hackathon? Here's a hint: It's not all about what you make. If you're stuck, go see what other people are up to. If you're tired, go to sleep. If you're hungry, find some free food. If you're bored, go do something fun. You don't even have to finish your project. As long as you're having fun and learning a few new things you're doing it right.
